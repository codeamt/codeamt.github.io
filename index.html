AnnMargaret Tutu

Product Engineer / AI Researcher

Biography

AnnMargaret is a Product Engineer for web and mobile and has over 4 years of experience deploying web projects as both a freelance developer and employee. Her language proficiencies span several object-oriented programming languages, including JavaScript, Ruby, Python and Java. Currently, she is completing an Andvanced Android Development Nanodegree as a 2017 Grow With Google Scholarship Award recipient where she also provides technical support to fellow scholars as an appointed Student Leader.

Her research project DeepBlock sits at the intersection of Deep Learning and Blockchain technology, exploring how ensemble methods for neural network training can simultaneously power blockchain software to validate transactions. Abstracts for this work have been submitted to workshops co-located at the upcoming NIPS 2018 conference hosted in Montreal.
