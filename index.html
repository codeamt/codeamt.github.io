Biography

Hey! I'm a Product/Software Engineer for Web 2.0, Web 3.0, and Native Mobile (Android), specializing in Blockchain technology and Machine/Deep Learning for big and small data. OOP languages I work with include JavaScript, Python, Java, and Ruby (along with the associated frameworks and tools for each).

My current research DeepBlock sits at the intersection of Deep Learning and Blockchain technology, introducing a blockchain-powered peer network service for decentralized training of models on mobile clients. This work aims to lower barriers to entry in both spaces and democratize the model convergence pipeline.
